My Favorite Place

My favorite place is East Gull Lake, located in Brainerd, Minnesota. My family has a cabin here and we spend most of our summers here. The beach is just a walk away from our cabin. I absolutely love going into the lake and spending time in the sun.

My Favorite Activities at the Lake

  • Swimming
  • Boating
  • Water Skiing
  • Wake Boarding
  • Tubing
  • Kayaking
  • Fishing
