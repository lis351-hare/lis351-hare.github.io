My Favorite TV Show

My favorite TV show is Friends, an American comedy sitcom. Friends features a group of companions who live in Manhattan, New York City. The group of friends all live different lives and work different jobs. This TV show has 10 total seasons, all of which are equally entertaining and funny!

My Favorite Characters from Friends

Monica:
- A funny cook who loves to keep her apartment clean.
Joey:
- An aspiring actor who dreams of having a huge impact on the cinematic community.
Chandler:
- A sarcastic man who works in statistical analysis and data reconfiguration.
Ross:
- A paleontologist who is very passionate about dinosaurs.
Rachel:
- A courageous woman who works multiple jobs throughout the show from a waitress to a personal shopper.
Phoebe:
- A hilarious songwriter and musician who performs for citizens all over New York City.
